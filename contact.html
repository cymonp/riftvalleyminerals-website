Important Notice

How to Verify It Is Really Us

If someone has contacted you claiming to represent Rift Valley Minerals, you can verify authenticity before proceeding:

  • All authorised email addresses end in @riftvalleyminerals.com. No gmail, yahoo, outlook, or similar free-mail address is authorised.
  • We never conduct commercial negotiations through WhatsApp, Telegram, or similar messaging platforms.
  • We never request advance payments, fees, commissions, or deposits from buyers.
  • We do not operate through brokers or "mandates" claiming to act on our behalf without signed authorisation.
  • To verify a specific person, write directly to compliance@riftvalleyminerals.com with the name, email address, and contact details of the person who has reached out to you. We will confirm or deny the relationship in writing.
