Packaging & Delivery

All gold is prepared for shipment under CCTV-monitored conditions at our secure facility and sealed in tamper-evident containers before hand-off to the accredited logistics provider.

  • Tamper-evident bar packaging with sequential numbering
  • Consolidated consignment manifests with buyer-side import refs
  • Export clearance through Entebbe International Airport
  • Secure logistics via Brinks, G4Si, or Loomis per buyer preference
  • Full insurance in transit covering declared value

Quality Assurance

Every shipment undergoes independent assay before export. We work with the major internationally recognised assayers operating in East Africa:

  • SGS Uganda
  • Alfred H Knight (AHK)
  • Alex Stewart International
  • Buyer-nominated independent assayer (upon request)

What Ships With Every Bar

  • Assay certificate — accredited third-party
  • Bar list — serial number, weight, purity
  • Commercial invoice — LBMA-referenced pricing
  • Certificate of origin — Uganda issue
  • Export permit — MEMD-issued
  • DGDA clearance — as applicable
  • Airway bill — accredited courier
  • Insurance certificate — in-transit cover
